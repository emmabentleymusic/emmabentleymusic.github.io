# Chamber Music Evening: Brahms and Mozart

## Performance Details
- **Date:** October 1, 2025
- **Venue:** St. Mary's Church
- **Ensemble:** With the renowned String Quartet Ensemble
- **Program:**
  - Mozart: Clarinet Quintet in A major, K. 581
  - Brahms: Clarinet Quintet in B minor, Op. 115

## Program Notes
This special evening of chamber music pairs two of the most beloved works in the clarinet quintet repertoire. Both pieces showcase the unique blend of clarinet with string quartet, highlighting the instrument's warmth and expressivity in intimate musical dialogue.

### Mozart Clarinet Quintet
Known as the "Stadler Quintet," this work was composed in 1789 for Anton Stadler. It exemplifies Mozart's mature style, combining virtuosity with profound musical expression.

# Mozart Clarinet Concerto in A major, K. 622

## Performance Details
- **Date:** September 15, 2025
- **Venue:** City Concert Hall
- **Ensemble:** City Symphony Orchestra
- **Conductor:** John Smith

## Program Notes
Mozart's Clarinet Concerto in A major, K. 622, was completed in 1791, just two months before the composer's death. Written for Anton Stadler, it is one of the earliest and most important concertos for clarinet. The work showcases the instrument's ability to play both lyrical passages and virtuosic runs, demonstrating the full range of the clarinet's capabilities.

## Movements
1. Allegro
2. Adagio
3. Rondo: Allegro

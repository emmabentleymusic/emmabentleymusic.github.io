Teaching Philosophy

I believe that music education is essential for developing well-rounded, creative, and empathetic individuals. My classroom is a safe and inclusive space where students are encouraged to explore, create, and perform with confidence. I strive to connect musical concepts to students' lives and cultures, fostering a personal and lasting appreciation for the arts.

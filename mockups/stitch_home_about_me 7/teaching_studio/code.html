My Teaching Philosophy

Student-Centered Learning

I believe in a student-centered approach, tailoring lessons to each individual's goals, learning style, and pace. By fostering a supportive and encouraging environment, I help students build confidence and discover their unique musical voice. This includes using differentiated instruction to meet every learner where they are.

Building Foundational Skills

My curriculum emphasizes strong fundamentals, including proper technique, music theory, and sight-reading. I use scaffolding techniques to break down complex concepts into manageable steps, ensuring a solid foundation for lifelong musical growth and enjoyment.

Fostering a Love for Music

Beyond technical proficiency, my ultimate goal is to instill a deep and lasting love for music. We explore diverse repertoire, learn about music history, and develop critical listening skills. My teaching style parallels effective classroom management by setting clear expectations and celebrating progress, making learning both disciplined and joyful.

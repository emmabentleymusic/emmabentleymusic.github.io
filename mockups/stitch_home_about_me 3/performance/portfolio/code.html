About Me

Performance Career

A passionate and dedicated musician with over 15 years of experience, I bring a wealth of knowledge to the stage. My performance career has taken me across the country, performing with renowned orchestras and chamber ensembles. I specialize in both classical and contemporary repertoire, always striving to connect with audiences through expressive and technically refined performances.

Teaching Philosophy

As an educator, my philosophy is rooted in fostering a deep and lasting love for music in every student. I believe in a personalized approach, tailoring lessons to individual learning styles and goals. Whether preparing for auditions, exploring new genres, or simply learning for enjoyment, I create a supportive environment that encourages growth, discipline, and artistic discovery.
